🧼 Keeping It Clean

We're not just about selling stuff; we're about helping you keep it in good shape. We've got washing guides, tips for stopping mold, and advice on picking the right liner. We want to help you keep your bathroom clean and healthy.

We suggest you:

*   Keep the curtain open after you shower so it dries faster.
*   Wash it or wipe it down to keep mold away.
*   Change liners every so often to keep things fresh.
*   Use cleaners that are good for the earth.

We want your stuff to last, so we reduce waste and keep your bathroom a happy place.
